The history of Coca-Cola

Coca-Cola is a carbonated beverage produced by the Coca-Cola Company on May 8, 1886. On May 8, 1886, John Pemberton concocted the original Coca-Cola drink and named it Pemberton Drink, but in 1891 Asa Candler purchased the Coca-Cola formula and founded the Coca-Cola Company. In 1899, Coca-Cola became popular with the glass bottle packaging, but the design of the glass bottle was still have some issues, and then the Coca-Cola Company tried to change the design of the Coca-Cola bottle.

The changes of bottles

In the history of Coca-Cola, the bottle packaging has undergone six changes, The years were 1899, 1900, 1915, 1916, 1957 and 1986
